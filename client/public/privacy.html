Introduction

Welcome to AutoTherapyNotes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-assisted note-taking, scheduling, and video conferencing services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profession, and account credentials.
  • Service Usage Information: Data related to your use of our platform, including notes taken, scheduling details, and meeting information.
  • AI-Generated Content: Notes and summaries created through our AI assistance features.
  • Audio and Video Data: Content from video conferences hosted on our platform.
  • Technical Information: IP address, device information, browser type, and usage statistics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process and complete transactions
  • To send administrative information, such as updates and security alerts
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • To develop new products and services
  • To generate anonymized, aggregated data for service improvements
  • To protect our rights, your rights, and the rights of others

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, destruction, use, modification, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, access controls, secure storage, and regular security assessments.

Special Provisions for Healthcare Professionals

AutoTherapyNotes recognizes that healthcare professionals are subject to specific privacy regulations, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We are committed to supporting your compliance with these regulations:

  • We act as a Business Associate as defined by HIPAA when providing services to covered entities.
  • We maintain administrative, physical, and technical safeguards that reasonably and appropriately protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the protected health information (PHI) that we process on behalf of healthcare professionals.
  • We only use and disclose PHI for the purpose of providing our services, as permitted by our Terms of Service and applicable BAAs, or as required by law.
  • We do not use PHI for marketing, advertising, or research purposes without explicit authorization.
  • Our AI systems that process clinical notes and other medical information are designed to keep this information confidential and are only used to provide the specific services requested by the healthcare professional.
  • We provide functionality to help healthcare professionals maintain detailed audit logs of all access to patient information.

Healthcare professionals who use our services should enter into a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with us before uploading or processing any protected health information. Please contact our compliance team to initiate this process.

AI Technology

Our AI note-taking assistance:

  • Processes content only for the purpose of providing the requested services
  • Does not use your content or derived information to train our AI models without explicit consent
  • Maintains appropriate technical safeguards for sensitive information
  • Allows you to review, edit, and delete AI-generated content
